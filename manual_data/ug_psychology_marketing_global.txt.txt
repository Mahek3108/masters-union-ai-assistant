UG Programme in Psychology & Marketing (Global – Illinois Tech)
Programme Overview
1. What is the UG Programme in Psychology & Marketing?

The UG Programme in Psychology & Marketing is a 4-year undergraduate programme that combines behavioural psychology with modern marketing practices.

2. What is the learning approach of this programme?

The programme follows a hands-on learning approach, where students apply psychology concepts to real-world marketing strategies and campaigns.

3. What is the format of the programme?

The programme is offered in a full-time format with on-campus learning.

4. Where is the programme conducted?

The programme is conducted across two international campuses:

Masters’ Union, Gurugram (India)

Illinois Institute of Technology, Chicago (USA)

5. What is the 3+1 programme structure?

The programme follows a 3+1 structure:

First 3 years: Study at Masters’ Union in Gurugram

Final year: Study at Illinois Institute of Technology in Chicago

6. When does the programme begin?

The programme commences in August 2026.

7. What is the Round 3 application deadline?

The Round 3 application deadline is 13th May 2026.

8. Who is eligible to apply for the programme?

Students who are:

Currently in Class XII, or

Pass-outs from 2024 onwards

are eligible to apply.

9. What is the duration of the programme?

The programme duration is 4 years.

10. What global opportunities do students get in this programme?

Students gain international academic exposure and global marketing insights by studying in both India and the United States.

Admissions – UG Programme in Psychology & Marketing (Global)
Application Process
1. What is the admission process for the Psychology & Marketing programme?

The admission process consists of four main stages:

Complete the Application

Scholarship Aptitude Test

Marketing Simulation & Interview

Admission Decision

Step 1 – Application Submission
2. What documents are required for the application?

Applicants must submit:

Class X marksheet

Class XI marksheet

Class XII passing certificate (for students graduating in 2024, 2025, or 2026)

3. Is a video essay required during the application?

Yes. Applicants must submit a video essay as part of the application process.

4. What are the requirements for the video essay?

The video essay must:

Be maximum 2 minutes long

Be uploaded to Google Drive

Have access set to “Anyone with the link can view”

5. What should students include in the video essay?

Applicants are encouraged to highlight:

Their unique hobbies and interests

Personal achievements

Qualities that make them memorable to the admissions committee

Step 2 – Scholarship Aptitude Test
6. What is the Scholarship Aptitude Test?

The Scholarship Aptitude Test is an online test conducted from home to evaluate students for scholarships and further shortlisting.

7. What is the duration of the Scholarship Test?

The test lasts 2 hours.

8. What topics are included in the Scholarship Test?

The test includes questions on:

Data analysis

Numerical ability

Logical reasoning

9. How long do students get to complete the scholarship test?

Students get 14 days to complete the online test after receiving the invitation.

10. Can standardized test scores replace the scholarship test?

Yes. Students who submit scores from exams such as:

JEE

IPMAT

SAT

CUET

may not need to take the scholarship aptitude test.

Step 3 – Marketing Simulation & Interview
11. What happens in the in-person marketing simulation?

Candidates participate in a business simulation exercise where they role-play as a CXO of a company.

12. What do students do during the simulation?

Students work in teams of 5–6 applicants to solve:

Marketing challenges

HR or business strategy problems

13. What is included in the interview stage?

Candidates have a one-on-one interview with faculty members or senior leadership.

14. Where are the simulation and interviews conducted?

These are conducted:

Across major cities in India

Or via Zoom in exceptional cases

Step 4 – Admission Decision
15. What admission outcomes are possible?

After evaluation, applicants may receive one of three outcomes:

Accepted

Rejected

Waitlisted

16. What happens if a student is accepted?

Accepted students receive:

Admission confirmation

Programme fee details

They must confirm admission by paying the admission fee within 7–21 days.

17. What happens if a student is waitlisted?

Waitlisted applicants may receive admission if additional seats become available.

18. Can rejected applicants apply again?

Yes. Rejected applicants can apply again in the next academic year.

Lateral Entry
19. Does the programme allow lateral entry?

Yes. Students who are currently studying in the first year at another university may apply for lateral entry into the programme.

20. Is the admission process different for lateral entry applicants?

No. The online admission process remains the same for all applicants.
