UG Programme in Finance & Economics (CA/CFA Pathway)
1. What is the UG Programme in Finance & Economics with CA/CFA Pathway?

The UG Programme in Finance & Economics is a 4-year bachelor’s programme that combines finance education with hands-on industry projects and provides a pathway to pursue CA (Chartered Accountant) or CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) qualifications.

2. What is the format of the Finance & Economics programme?

The programme is offered in a full-time format with an opt-in residential option.

3. When does the programme commence?

The programme is scheduled to start in August 2026.

4. What is the Round 3 application deadline?

The Round 3 application deadline is 13th May 2026.

5. Who is eligible to apply for the programme?

Students who are:

Currently in Class XII, or

Pass-outs from 2024 onwards

are eligible to apply.

6. What is the duration of the programme?

The programme duration is 4 years.

7. Does the programme include professional certification pathways?

Yes. The programme includes pathways that allow students to pursue professional certifications such as:

CA (Chartered Accountant)

CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst)

8. What kind of learning approach does the programme follow?

The programme focuses on hands-on learning, industry projects, and real-world financial applications rather than only theoretical coursework.

9. Is residential accommodation mandatory for the programme?

No. The programme offers an opt-in residential option, meaning students can choose whether to stay in campus housing.
