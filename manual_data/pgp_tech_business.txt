PGP in Technology & Business Management (PGP TBM)
Programme Overview
1. What is the PGP in Technology and Business Management?

The PGP in Technology & Business Management (PGP TBM) is a full-time postgraduate programme designed to develop business leaders with strong technology understanding.

It combines:

Business strategy

Technology management

Real-world industry projects

Leadership training

The programme is offered by Masters’ Union in Gurugram.

2. Where is the programme conducted?

The programme takes place at the Masters’ Union campus in Gurugram, located in DLF Cyberpark.

3. When does the programme start?

The programme begins in June 2026 for the Cohort of 2027.

4. Is the programme residential?

The programme is full-time with optional residential accommodation.

Programme Variants

Masters’ Union offers two versions of the PGP TBM programme.

PGP TBM (For Experienced Professionals)
5. What is the duration of the PGP TBM programme?

The PGP TBM programme lasts 16 months.

6. Who is eligible for the PGP TBM programme?

Applicants must have more than 1 year of work experience before the MBA.

7. How many credits are included in the programme?

The programme includes 120 credits, combining:

In-class learning

Out-class practical projects

8. Is internship mandatory in PGP TBM?

No. Internships are optional for the experienced professionals cohort.

9. How is the academic structure organised?

The programme includes 8 academic terms, each lasting 1–2 months.

10. What is the average salary after completing PGP TBM?

The average post-MBA salary (CTC) is approximately ₹33.39 lakh per year.

11. What is the highest salary offered to graduates?

The highest CTC recorded is ₹1.28 crore per year.

PGP TBM YLC (Young Leaders Cohort)
12. What is the PGP TBM YLC programme?

The PGP TBM Young Leaders Cohort (YLC) is designed for fresh graduates or early-career professionals.

It focuses more on foundational business and technology concepts.

13. What is the duration of the PGP TBM YLC programme?

The PGP TBM YLC programme lasts 24 months.

14. Who is eligible for the Young Leaders Cohort?

Applicants with 0–1 year of work experience are eligible.

15. How many credits are included in the YLC programme?

The programme includes 200 credits, combining:

Classroom learning

Practical industry projects

16. Are internships mandatory in PGP TBM YLC?

Yes. Internships are mandatory for the Young Leaders Cohort.

17. How long are the academic terms in the YLC programme?

The programme includes 8 terms, each lasting 3–4 months.

18. What is the average salary after the YLC programme?

The average CTC after graduation is approximately ₹28.24 lakh per year.

19. What is the highest salary offered in the YLC programme?

The highest salary offered is ₹46.22 lakh per year.

PGP TBM Admissions – Question Answer Format
Application Timeline

Q1. What is the early evaluation deadline for the PGP TBM programme?
The early evaluation application deadline is 31 March 2026.

Q2. What is the final application deadline for the PGP TBM programme?
The final application deadline is 3 May 2026.

Q3. What is the application fee for the programme?
The application fee is ₹1,500.

Q4. When will the MU-BAAT test be conducted for early evaluation applicants?
For early evaluation applicants, the MU-BAAT deadline is 5 April 2026.

Q5. When will the personal interview take place for early evaluation applicants?
The personal interview is scheduled for 8 April 2026.

Q6. When will the MU-BAAT test be conducted for final evaluation applicants?
For final evaluation applicants, the MU-BAAT deadline is 7 May 2026.

Q7. When will the personal interview take place for final evaluation applicants?
The personal interview is scheduled for 10 May 2026.

Application Process

Q8. What are the steps in the admission process for PGP TBM?
The admission process includes four stages:

Completing the application form

Taking the MU-BAAT test (if required)

Personal interview

Final admission decision

Application Form

Q9. What documents are required while submitting the application form?

Applicants must submit:

Latest resume

Academic qualification documents

Work experience documents

Extracurricular achievements

CAT / GMAT / GMAT Focus scores (optional)

Q10. Is CAT or GMAT mandatory for admission?
No, CAT or GMAT scores are optional. If applicants do not submit these scores, they must take the MU-BAAT test.

MU-BAAT Test

Q11. What is MU-BAAT?
MU-BAAT stands for Masters’ Union Business Aptitude and Admissions Test.

It evaluates:

Communication skills

Business aptitude

Analytical thinking

Creativity

Clarity of thought

Q12. Who needs to take the MU-BAAT test?
Applicants who do not submit CAT or GMAT scores must take the MU-BAAT test.

Q13. What is the duration of the MU-BAAT test?
The test lasts 45–60 minutes.

Q14. What is the format of the MU-BAAT test?
The test includes:

9 questions

5 sections

Online recorded responses

Short breaks between sections

Q15. Do students need to prepare for MU-BAAT?
No specific preparation is required. The test focuses on personal insights, creativity, and business thinking.

Personal Interview

Q16. Who conducts the personal interview?
The interview is conducted by Masters’ Union faculty members and industry leaders.

Q17. When do applicants receive interview details?
Applicants receive interview details 3–5 days before the scheduled interview date.

Admission Decision

Q18. What are the possible admission outcomes?

Applicants may receive one of three outcomes:

Accepted

Waitlisted

Rejected

Q19. What happens if an applicant is accepted?
Accepted applicants receive the admission offer and fee details and must confirm their admission within the deadline mentioned in the offer letter.

Q20. What happens if an applicant is waitlisted?
Waitlisted applicants may receive admission if seats become available. Masters’ Union does not maintain a ranked waitlist.

Q21. Can rejected applicants apply again in the same year?
No, rejected applicants cannot apply again in the same academic year but can apply in the next academic year.

Programme Fees

Q22. What is the admission fee for the programme?
The admission fee is ₹90,000 (partially refundable).

Q23. What is the tuition fee for the programme?
The tuition fee is ₹32,75,384.

Q24. What is the total programme fee?
The total fee is ₹33,65,384.

Q25. Is GST applicable on the programme fees?
No, GST is not applicable because Masters’ Union is registered with the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC).

Hostel Accommodation

Q26. Where are the hostels located?
Hostels are located in Nathupur, near the Masters’ Union campus.

Q27. What are the hostel accommodation options?

Students can choose between:

Single occupancy room

Double occupancy room

Q28. What is the cost of single occupancy hostel accommodation?
The cost for single occupancy is ₹5,25,000 for 12 months.

Q29. What is the cost of double occupancy hostel accommodation?
The cost for double occupancy is ₹3,35,000 for 12 months.

Q30. What amenities are included in the hostel?

Hostel facilities include:

Internet access

Shuttle service to campus

Study areas

Recreation area

Cafeteria

Gym

Food is not included in the hostel fee.

Q31. Is a security deposit required for hostel accommodation?
Yes, students must pay a ₹50,000 refundable security deposit.
