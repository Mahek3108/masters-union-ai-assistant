The Entrepreneurship & Business Acceleration Program (EBAP) is a 6-month hybrid programme designed for Indian business owners, entrepreneurs, SMEs, MSMEs, and family-run businesses who want to scale operations, increase sales, and automate business processes.

Unlike traditional management courses, EBAP focuses on practical business implementation rather than theoretical concepts. Participants learn strategies that can be applied directly within their existing businesses to improve profitability, streamline operations, and build sustainable growth.

Curriculum Structure

The EBAP curriculum is organised into five practical modules, each focused on solving real operational challenges faced by Indian businesses.

Module 1: Increase Sales

This module focuses on expanding revenue streams and building stronger sales systems.

Participants learn how to:

Use WhatsApp and Instagram marketing to reach Indian consumers

Design smart incentives for sales teams without relying on discounts

Expand into Tier-2 and Tier-3 city markets

Transition from informal selling (“jugaad selling”) to value-based selling

Upsell to existing loyal customers

Partner with distributors, kirana stores, and e-commerce platforms

Improve branding through packaging and positioning strategies

Build simple sales dashboards to track performance in real time

Module 2: Maximize Profits

This module helps business owners improve profitability by managing costs, financing, and operations more effectively.

Participants learn how to:

Access funding from banks, NBFCs, and angel investors

Negotiate better terms with suppliers

Understand the true cost structure of products, including hidden expenses

Transition to digital payments to reduce cash leakages

Improve payment collections and reduce delays

Use government schemes and MSME subsidies to support business growth

Module 3: Prevent Leakages

Many businesses lose profits due to operational inefficiencies. This module focuses on identifying and eliminating these leakages.

Participants learn how to:

Implement simple CRM systems to track leads and customers

Use software tools such as Tally, Zoho, and Vyapar to monitor business operations

Map business processes to identify inefficiencies

Apply lean principles to reduce waste and delays

Digitize inventory tracking instead of relying on manual records

Module 4: Business Automation

This module focuses on transforming owner-driven businesses into structured organisations.

Participants learn how to:

Delegate non-core tasks such as accounting, HR, and logistics

Hire professional managers while maintaining control over the business

Develop Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) to standardize operations

Build clear organizational structures and reporting hierarchies

Use AI tools such as ChatGPT and automation platforms like Zapier to streamline workflows

Implement professional hiring practices instead of informal recruitment

Hire and manage a professional CEO

Create Management Information System (MIS) dashboards for quick decision-making

Define decision rules so every small issue does not require owner approval

Transition from an operator role to an owner and strategic investor

Module 5: Family Harmony

Many Indian businesses are family-run. This module focuses on governance and conflict management within family enterprises.

Participants learn how to:

Create clear succession plans and wills

Develop a family constitution outlining rules for participation and compensation

Train the next generation to manage the business effectively

Separate family matters from business decision-making

Set fair compensation structures for family members

Use independent advisors to mediate conflicts

Resolve disputes between family members through defined roles and governance structures

Establish a family council to address issues before they affect operations

Balance family traditions with professional business practices

Build long-term family business legacies across generations

Entrepreneurship & Business Acceleration Program (EBAP)
Detailed Curriculum Structure

The Entrepreneurship & Business Acceleration Program (EBAP) delivers a structured curriculum designed to help Indian business owners build scalable systems, professionalise management, and accelerate long-term growth. The programme is divided into ten practical modules, each addressing a critical aspect of building and sustaining a successful business.

Module 1: Run Your Business on Autopilot

This module focuses on building systems and structures that allow a business to operate smoothly without requiring constant owner intervention.

Participants learn how to:

Define a clear vision, mission, and values for the organisation

Translate long-term goals into a practical one-year execution plan

Track sales, costs, and operational performance through simple metrics

Diagnose and fix performance issues quickly when results fall short

Design operational processes that ensure business continuity even when the owner is not actively involved

The objective is to help entrepreneurs move from owner-driven operations to system-driven businesses.

Module 2: People Who Deliver (Family & Non-Family)

People management is a key factor in scaling a business. This module focuses on building strong teams while maintaining harmony between family and professional staff.

Participants learn how to:

Design fair compensation, promotion, and reward systems

Hire and retain talented employees who contribute to long-term growth

Define professional roles and responsibilities for family members

Delegate effectively without excessive micromanagement

Develop next-generation leaders capable of taking responsibility for the business

This module ensures businesses are supported by capable and accountable teams.

Module 3: Tech Made Simple — Tools That Save Time & Money

Technology can significantly improve operational efficiency when implemented correctly. This module focuses on practical digital tools that simplify business management.

Participants learn how to:

Implement ERP and CRM systems to manage operations from a single platform

Use dashboards to monitor sales, costs, and profits in real time

Apply AI-driven tools for forecasting, customer insights, and inventory planning

Leverage digital solutions to accelerate growth and improve decision-making

Enhance customer experience through digital engagement channels

The aim is to make technology accessible and practical for traditional businesses.

Module 4: Profits, Money & Governance

This module focuses on financial discipline and governance structures that support sustainable growth.

Participants learn how to:

Interpret financial data to identify hidden profit leakages

Manage cash flow and working capital efficiently

Raise capital through banks, private equity, or public markets

Introduce governance practices without compromising family control

Increase enterprise value and prepare the business for long-term expansion

By the end of this module, participants gain a clearer understanding of financial health and strategic growth planning.

Module 5: Building New Growth Engines

Businesses must continuously innovate and explore new opportunities. This module focuses on identifying and launching new ventures or revenue streams.

Participants learn how to:

Identify promising new business opportunities

Design and launch new products or services quickly

Acquire the first set of customers in a new venture

Raise early-stage capital if required

Scale successful ventures or exit strategically without jeopardizing the core business

The goal is to encourage entrepreneurs to develop multiple growth engines for their companies.

Module 6: Sales & Marketing That Work

This module focuses on building reliable sales systems and effective marketing strategies.

Participants learn how to:

Design go-to-market (GTM) strategies across channels such as D2C, distributors, and franchises

Combine digital and offline marketing to achieve measurable returns

Build strong brand equity that fosters long-term customer trust

Develop structured sales pipelines rather than relying on chance

Track and improve sales team productivity using simple dashboards

The emphasis is on predictable and scalable revenue generation.

Module 7: Innovation, Leadership & Legacy

Entrepreneurs must lead businesses through change while protecting long-term family values. This module addresses leadership development and strategic thinking.

Participants learn how to:

Drive innovation in products, services, and processes

Negotiate effectively with vendors, customers, and internal stakeholders

Lead teams during uncertain or rapidly changing market conditions

Balance growth ambitions with preservation of family legacy

Build a culture that promotes sustainability and long-term success

This module strengthens the leadership mindset required to sustain multigenerational businesses.

Module 8: Cost Control & Supply Chain Excellence

Operational efficiency and cost management are essential for maintaining profitability.

Participants learn how to:

Reduce operational waste and unnecessary expenses

Manage vendor relationships to secure better pricing and reliability

Improve productivity across manufacturing and service operations

Establish service standards that ensure customer satisfaction

Prevent operational disruptions through proactive maintenance strategies

The focus is on creating efficient, resilient operational systems.

Module 9: Managing Self & Family

Running a business also requires strong personal leadership and effective family governance.

Participants learn how to:

Build self-awareness and emotional intelligence as a leader

Manage stress, time, and personal energy effectively

Define clear rules for family participation in the business

Resolve conflicts between family and professional team members

Maintain a healthy balance between personal life and business responsibilities

This module helps entrepreneurs strengthen both personal leadership and family harmony.

Module 10: Protecting Your Business in Tough Times

Businesses must be prepared for uncertainty, economic downturns, and unexpected crises.

Participants learn how to:

Identify operational and financial risks before they escalate

Develop crisis management plans that ensure continuity

Maintain regulatory compliance and avoid legal complications

Protect profits through insurance, hedging strategies, and financial buffers

Safeguard brand reputation and customer trust during challenging situations

The objective is to build businesses that are resilient and capable of surviving disruptions.
