Programme: Undergraduate Programme in Technology & Business Management
1. What is the Undergraduate Programme in Technology & Business Management?

The Undergraduate Programme in Technology & Business Management is a 4-year full-time degree programme designed to combine technology knowledge with business management skills, including 1 year of industry training.

2. What is the format of the programme?

The programme is offered in a Full-Time format with an opt-in residential option.

3. When does the programme commence?

The programme is scheduled to commence in August 2026.

4. What is the Round 3 application deadline?

The Round 3 application deadline is 13th May 2026.

5. Who is eligible to apply for this programme?

Students who are:

Currently in Class XII, or

Pass-outs from 2024 onwards

are eligible to apply.

6. What is the duration of the programme?

The programme duration is 4 years, which includes 1 year of industry training.

7. Does the programme include industry training?

Yes. The programme includes 1 year of industry training, allowing students to gain practical work experience.

8. Is residential stay mandatory?

No. The programme offers an opt-in residential option, meaning students can choose whether they want to stay on campus.

9. What kind of learning experience does the programme provide?

The programme combines academic learning with industry exposure, helping students develop technical, business, and practical skills.



Industry Experts & Faculty
1. Who teaches the courses in this programme?

The courses are taught by 200+ industry leaders including CXOs and Managing Directors from leading companies such as Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Meta, Kia, and Epigamia.

2. Are industry professionals involved in teaching?

Yes. The programme is taught by empaneled industry experts including CXOs and MDs from top companies like Amazon, Airtel, PayPal, Mastercard, Swiggy, and Samsung.

3. How many industry experts are involved in teaching?

The programme includes 100+ empaneled industry experts who actively contribute to teaching and mentoring students.

4. What kind of learning experience do students get from industry leaders?

Students participate in live case studies, mentorship sessions, and industry-led workshops conducted by senior leaders from top companies.

5. Do students get mentorship from industry leaders?

Yes. Students receive direct mentorship from Managing Directors and senior business leaders.

Hands-on Business Education
6. What makes the learning approach unique?

The programme focuses on hands-on business education, where students learn directly from leaders working at companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Airtel, and American Express.

7. Are there live business case studies in the programme?

Yes. Students work on live case studies with CXOs from leading firms.

8. Does the programme include workshops?

Yes. The programme includes industry-led workshops and immersive projects to provide practical learning experience.

Startup & Entrepreneurship Opportunities
9. Can students start their own business during the programme?

Yes. Students can build and scale their own e-commerce ventures through a dropshipping model during the programme.

10. What revenue targets are expected from student ventures?

Students are encouraged to target revenue of ₹1 crore or more through their startups.

11. How successful have student startups been?

Students have:

Generated ₹3 crore+ in revenue

Launched 60+ startups

Built a global customer base of over 10,000 people

12. Do students receive support for entrepreneurship?

Yes. Students receive guidance, mentorship, and industry exposure to help them launch and scale their startups.

Internships

Students can start internships from the first year of college.

14. Which companies offer internships to students?

15. What percentage of students get internships in the first year?

Approximately 75% of students secure internships within their first year.

Yes. Students have worked across India, UAE, US, and Singapore.

Admissions Process – Undergraduate Programme in Technology & Business Management
Application Process
1. How can I apply for the programme?

Students need to complete the online application form and submit the required documents, followed by the admission evaluation process.

2. What documents are required for the application?

Applicants need to submit:

Class X marksheet

Class XI marksheet

Class XII passing certificate (for students graduating in 2024, 2025, or 2026)

JEE (Session 1 or Session 2) or SAT scores

A video essay

3. What is the video essay requirement?

Applicants must submit a video essay of up to 2 minutes highlighting their personality, hobbies, and unique qualities.

4. When should the video essay be submitted?

The video must be submitted within 7 days after completing Part 1 of the application and paying the application fee.

5. How should the video essay be submitted?

Applicants must:

Upload the video to Google Drive

Set sharing to “Anyone with the link can view”

Upload the link in Part 2 of the application form

Scholarship Aptitude Test
6. What happens after the application is shortlisted?

Shortlisted applicants receive an online scholarship aptitude test.

7. What is the duration of the scholarship test?

The scholarship test lasts 2 hours.

8. What topics are included in the aptitude test?

The test includes questions on:

Data Analysis

Numerical Ability

Logical Reasoning

9. How much time do students get to complete the scholarship test?

Students get 14 days to complete the online scholarship test.

10. Is the scholarship test mandatory?

No. Students who submit JEE, IPMAT, SAT, or CUET scores may not need to take the scholarship test.

Business Simulation & Interview
11. What is the business simulation round?

Candidates participate in a team-based business simulation, where they role-play as CXOs of a company and solve real business challenges.

12. How many students participate in the business simulation?

The simulation is conducted in teams of 5–6 applicants.

13. What happens after the simulation?

Candidates attend a 1-on-1 interview with faculty members or senior leadership.

14. Where are the simulation and interview conducted?

These rounds are conducted in major cities across India, and in special cases via Zoom.

15. Are the simulation and interview on the same day?

Yes. Both the business simulation and interview are scheduled on the same day.

Admission Decision
16. How are admission decisions communicated?

After evaluation, applicants are informed of their admission status.

17. What are the possible admission outcomes?

Applicants may receive one of three decisions:

Accepted

Rejected

Waitlisted

18. What happens if a student is accepted?

Accepted applicants receive:

Admission offer

Programme financial details

They must confirm admission within 7–21 days by paying the admission fee.

19. Can rejected applicants apply again?

Yes. Rejected applicants can apply again in the next academic year.

20. What does waitlisted mean?

Waitlisted applicants may receive admission if seats become available later.

Masters’ Union does not maintain a ranked waitlist.

Lateral Entry (Transfer Students)
21. Can students transfer from another university?

Yes. Masters’ Union offers a Lateral Entry pathway.

22. Who is eligible for lateral entry?

Students currently in their first year at another university can transfer to the programme.

23. Is the admission process different for lateral entry?

No. The same online admission process applies to all applicants.

Fee Structure
24. What is the total programme tuition fee?

The total tuition fee for the 4-year programme is INR 52,50,000, plus a non-refundable admission fee of INR 1,80,000.

25. What are the tuition fees for each year?
Year	Tuition Fee
Year 1	INR 10,80,000
Year 2	INR 15,95,000
Year 3	INR 17,85,000
Year 4	INR 7,90,000
26. Does the programme have an admission fee?

Yes. There is a non-refundable admission fee of INR 1,80,000.

27. Can the fourth-year fee change?

Yes. The fourth-year fee may vary if a student studies abroad or chooses the PGP pathway.

Hostel Fees
28. What are the hostel accommodation options?

Two accommodation types are available:

Twin Sharing

Single Sharing

29. What is the hostel fee for twin sharing?

Twin sharing accommodation costs INR 27,500 per month.

30. What is the hostel fee for single sharing?

Single sharing accommodation costs INR 48,500 per month.
