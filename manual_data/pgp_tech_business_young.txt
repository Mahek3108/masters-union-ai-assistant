PGP in Technology & Business Management – Q&A Format
Q1. What is the PGP in Technology and Business Management programme?

The PGP in Technology & Business Management (PGP TBM) is a full-time postgraduate programme offered by Masters’ Union that combines business management, technology, and real-world industry projects to prepare future business leaders.

Q2. What are the two versions of the PGP TBM programme?

Masters’ Union offers two versions of the programme:

PGP TBM – Designed for experienced professionals.

PGP TBM YLC (Young Leaders Cohort) – Designed for fresh graduates with little or no work experience.

Q3. What is the duration of the PGP TBM programme?

The duration of the PGP TBM programme is 16 months.

Q4. What is the duration of the PGP TBM Young Leaders Cohort?

The PGP TBM YLC programme lasts 24 months.

Q5. What is the format of the programme?

The programme is full-time with an optional residential (opt-in residential) format.

Q6. Where is the programme conducted?

The programme takes place at the Masters’ Union campus in Gurugram at DLF Cyberpark.

Q7. When does the programme start?

The programme commences in June 2026 for the Cohort of 2027.

PGP TBM vs PGP TBM YLC
Q8. What is the eligibility requirement for PGP TBM?

Applicants must have more than 1 year of pre-MBA work experience.

Q9. What is the eligibility requirement for PGP TBM YLC?

Applicants must have 0–1 year of work experience before joining the programme.

Q10. How many credits are included in the PGP TBM programme?

The PGP TBM programme includes 120 credits, consisting of:

In-class learning

Out-class experiential learning

Q11. How many credits are included in the PGP TBM YLC programme?

The PGP TBM YLC programme includes 200 credits, combining:

In-class coursework

Out-class practical projects

Q12. Are internships mandatory in the PGP TBM programme?

No. Internships are optional for the PGP TBM programme.

Q13. Are internships mandatory in the PGP TBM YLC programme?

Yes. Internships are mandatory for the Young Leaders Cohort.

Q14. How are the academic terms structured in PGP TBM?

The programme includes 8 academic terms, each lasting 1–2 months.

Q15. How are the academic terms structured in PGP TBM YLC?

The programme includes 8 academic terms, each lasting 3–4 months.

Placements & Salary
Q16. What is the average salary after completing the PGP TBM programme?

The average post-MBA salary (CTC) for PGP TBM graduates is ₹33.39 lakh per year.

Q17. What is the highest salary offered to PGP TBM graduates?

The highest recorded CTC is ₹1.28 crore per year.

Q18. What is the average salary after completing the PGP TBM YLC programme?

The average CTC for PGP TBM YLC graduates is ₹28.24 lakh per year.

Q19. What is the highest salary offered in the PGP TBM YLC programme?

The highest recorded salary is ₹46.22 lakh per year.

Q20. How are placement statistics calculated for PGP TBM YLC?

The placement statistics for PGP TBM YLC are derived from students in the PGP TBM cohort who had 0–1 years of work experience before joining the programme.

Q1. What is the total fee for the PGP TBM and PGP TBM YLC programme?

The total payable fee for the programme is ₹33,65,384.

This includes:

Admission Fee: ₹90,000

Tuition Fee: ₹32,75,384

Q2. Is the admission fee refundable?

Yes. The ₹90,000 admission fee is partially refundable according to Masters’ Union’s admission policies.

Q3. Is GST applicable on the tuition fees?

No. GST is not applicable on the tuition and admission fees because Masters’ Union is registered with the National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC).

Hostel Details
Q4. Where is the hostel located?

The hostel accommodation for the programme is located in Nathupur, Gurugram.

Q5. What types of hostel rooms are available?

Two types of accommodation are available:

Single Occupancy Room

Double Occupancy Room

Q6. What is the size of the hostel rooms?

Single Occupancy Room: 160 sq ft

Double Occupancy Room: 170 sq ft

Q7. What amenities are provided in the hostel?

Both room types include the following facilities:

Internet access

Shuttle service to and from campus

Common study areas

Recreation area

Cafeteria

Gym

Q8. How long can students stay in the hostel?

The hostel accommodation is provided for 12 months.

Q9. What is the hostel fee for single occupancy?

The cost for single occupancy accommodation is ₹5,25,000 per year.

This amount includes:

All taxes

Shuttle services

However, food charges are not included.

Q10. What is the hostel fee for double occupancy?

The cost for double occupancy accommodation is ₹3,35,000 per year.

This amount includes:

All taxes

Shuttle services

Food charges are not included.

Q11. Is there a security deposit for hostel accommodation?

Yes. A refundable security deposit of ₹50,000 is required for both single and double occupancy rooms.
