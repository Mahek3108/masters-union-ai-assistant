PGP Rise: General Management (Global)

The PGP Rise: General Management (Global) is a career reset programme for mid-career professionals who want to transition into senior leadership roles, global management positions, or entrepreneurial ventures. The programme combines online learning with international business exposure, enabling participants to develop global management perspectives while continuing their professional careers.

Programme Overview
Duration

12 months

Format

Fully online learning, combined with three global immersion experiences at international business and industry locations.

Commencement Date

To Be Determined (Tentative)

Eligibility

The programme is designed for working professionals, particularly those seeking to accelerate their leadership journey or transition into global management roles.

Global Learning Experience

A key feature of the programme is its international exposure, which allows participants to learn from global institutions and business leaders.

One of the primary global immersion experiences takes place at the INSEAD campus in France, one of the world’s most renowned business schools.

During this immersion, participants:

Attend sessions at the INSEAD France campus

Engage with international faculty and industry experts

Experience European business culture and global leadership practices

Participants also gain exposure to leading multinational organisations through company visits, including headquarters of companies such as Louis Vuitton and Schneider Electric.

These immersive experiences provide participants with an understanding of global markets, leadership practices, and international business strategy.

Leadership Development & Mentorship

Beyond traditional management education, the programme emphasizes personalised leadership coaching.

Participants receive:

Individual coaching from leadership experts

Guidance from mentors who have coached CXOs and senior executives of global organisations

Structured development of leadership skills, strategic thinking, and executive decision-making

This coaching component is designed to help participants transition from operational roles to strategic leadership positions.

Learning Approach

The programme blends multiple learning formats to ensure practical and applied knowledge.

Participants benefit from:

Interactive online classes

Global immersion programmes

Industry visits and business exposure

Leadership coaching sessions

Networking with international peers and executives

This structure enables professionals to gain global management knowledge without pausing their careers.

Career Outcomes

Graduates of the programme aim to move into roles such as:

Senior leadership and management positions

Global strategy and consulting roles

Entrepreneurship and business leadership

Executive roles within multinational organisations

The programme focuses on developing professionals capable of leading organisations in complex and international business environments.

Curriculum Overview

The PGP Rise: General Management (Global) curriculum follows a structured four-term format designed to combine classroom learning with real-world business application. Participants build foundational management knowledge in early terms and progressively move toward advanced strategy, leadership, and industry exposure.

The programme integrates In-Class learning, OutClass experiences, industry immersions, and hands-on entrepreneurial challenges.

PGP Rise: General Management (Global)
Application Timeline & Admission Process

The PGP Rise: General Management (Global) programme follows a structured multi-stage admission process designed to evaluate applicants’ professional experience, leadership potential, and career goals.

The admissions journey typically includes three key stages: application submission, interview evaluation, and final admission decision.

Application Rounds

Admissions are conducted across multiple rounds.

Round	Application Deadline	Interview Period	Application Fee	Status
Round 3	TBD	TBD	$59	Active
Round 2	TBD	TBD	$59	Closed
Round 1	TBD	TBD	$59	Closed

Applicants must submit their application within the active round to be considered.

Step 1: Complete the Online Application

Applicants begin the admissions process by filling out an online application form.

Required Information

Candidates are required to provide:

Personal details

Professional details

Latest resume

Essay Requirement

Applicants must also submit an essay as part of the application. They can choose between two formats:

Video Essay – approximately 2 minutes

Written Essay – up to 200 words

The essay helps the admissions committee understand the applicant’s career goals, motivations, and leadership perspective.

Step 2: Online Interview

Shortlisted applicants are invited to participate in an online interview.

Purpose of the Interview

The interview helps the admissions committee assess:

Professional experience and leadership potential

Strategic thinking and career aspirations

Alignment with the programme’s objectives

Interview Panel

The panel typically includes:

Academic faculty members

Industry professionals and executives

Members of the Masters’ Union programme team

The session also allows applicants to learn more about the programme structure, global immersions, and career opportunities.

Step 3: Final Admission Decision

After the interview, the admissions office reviews the candidate’s complete application profile.

The final evaluation considers:

Application form and resume

Essay submission

Interview performance

Overall fit for the programme

The Admissions Committee makes the final decision.

Applicants are informed of the outcome through official email communication.
