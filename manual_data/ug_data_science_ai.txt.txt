Undergraduate Programme in Data Science & AI
1. What is the Undergraduate Programme in Data Science & AI?

The Undergraduate Programme in Data Science & AI is a 4-year full-time programme designed to teach students data science, artificial intelligence, and real-world industry applications.

2. What is the format of the Data Science & AI programme?

The programme is offered in a full-time format with an opt-in residential option.

3. When does the programme begin?

The programme is scheduled to commence in August 2026.

4. What is the Round 3 application deadline?

The Round 3 application deadline is 13th May 2026.

5. Who is eligible to apply for the Data Science & AI programme?

Students who are:

Currently in Class XII, or

Pass-outs from recent years

and have studied Mathematics are eligible to apply.

6. Is Mathematics required for this programme?

Yes. Applicants must have studied Mathematics in Class XII to be eligible for the programme.

7. What is the duration of the Data Science & AI programme?

The programme duration is 4 years.

8. Does the programme include industry training?

Yes. The programme includes 1 year of industry training where students gain practical experience working with companies.

9. Is residential accommodation mandatory?

No. The programme offers an opt-in residential option, meaning students can choose whether they want to stay in campus housing.

10. What skills do students learn in the Data Science & AI programme?

Students develop skills in areas such as:

Data analysis

Machine learning

Artificial intelligence

Programming and data tools

Business applications of AI

Programme Learning Approach

The programme follows an industry-integrated curriculum where students learn by building real AI applications instead of focusing only on theoretical grades.

2. How is the curriculum structured across semesters?

Each year is structured into two phases:

Semester 1: Develop AI tools

Semester 2: Apply AI solutions in real-world industry problems

Curriculum – Undergraduate Programme in Data Science & AI
Year 1 – Learn the Fundamentals of Data Science & AI
1. What is the focus of Year 1 in the Data Science & AI programme?

Year 1 focuses on building foundational knowledge in data science, programming, and artificial intelligence to help students understand how data powers modern business decisions.

2. What core subjects are taught in Year 1?

Year 1 includes foundational courses such as:

Introduction to Data Science

Linear Algebra

Probability & Statistics

Programming Fundamentals

Computer Fundamentals

Data Structures

Unsupervised Machine Learning

3. Do students learn programming in the first year?

Yes. Students learn programming through courses like:

Programming Fundamentals I

Programming Fundamentals II

Coding with Java

These courses help students understand logic, algorithms, and software development for AI systems.

4. What AI concepts are introduced in the first year?

Students learn about:

Origins of Artificial Intelligence

AI Agents

Industry transformation through AI

5. Do students work on practical AI projects in Year 1?

Yes. Students participate in projects such as:

Applied AI Challenge – building AI products and generating ₹5 lakh or more in revenue.

AI Application in Industry – building AI solutions for real companies using techniques like clustering and anomaly detection.

95 percentile or higher in JEE, or

40+ score in MUSAT

7. What exam scores are required for an 80% scholarship?

Students may qualify for an 80% scholarship with:

99 percentile or higher in JEE, or

1590+ in SAT, or

43+ score in MUSAT
