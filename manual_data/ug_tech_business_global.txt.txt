UG Programme in Technology & Business Management (3+1 Dual Degree)
Programme Overview
1. What is the UG Programme in Technology & Business Management?

The UG Programme in Technology & Business Management is a 4-year undergraduate programme that combines technology knowledge with practical business education.

2. What makes this programme unique?

The programme follows a “learn business by doing business” approach, where students work on real-world business challenges and projects.

3. What is the 3+1 dual degree structure?

The programme follows a 3+1 structure:

First 3 years: Study at Masters’ Union in India

Final year: Study at Illinois Institute of Technology (Illinois Tech), Chicago

4. What degree do students receive after completing the programme?

Students graduate with a Bachelor’s degree through the dual-degree pathway with Illinois Tech.

5. What global exposure does the programme offer?

Students gain international academic exposure and industry insights by studying in both India and the United States.

6. What skills do students learn in this programme?

Students develop skills in areas such as:

Business strategy

Technology management

Entrepreneurship

Data-driven decision making

Product management

7. What is the main learning philosophy of the programme?

The programme emphasises experiential learning, where students learn through projects, startups, industry mentorship, and business simulations.

8. Who is this programme suitable for?

The programme is ideal for students who want to build careers in:

Business management

Technology startups

Consulting

Product management

Entrepreneurship


UG Programme in Technology & Business Management (Global – Illinois Tech)
Programme Overview
1. What is the Undergraduate Programme in Technology & Business Management?

The Undergraduate Programme in Technology & Business Management is a 4-year full-time programme designed to teach business strategy, technology management, and entrepreneurship through practical learning.

2. What is the format of the programme?

The programme is offered in a full-time format with on-campus learning.

3. Where is the programme conducted?

The programme is conducted across two global campuses:

Gurugram, India – Masters’ Union campus

Chicago, USA – Illinois Institute of Technology

4. What is the 3+1 programme structure?

The programme follows a 3+1 structure:

First 3 years: Study at Masters’ Union in Gurugram

Final year: Study at Illinois Institute of Technology in Chicago

5. When does the programme commence?

The programme is scheduled to begin in August 2026.

6. What is the Round 3 application deadline?

The Round 3 application deadline is 13th May 2026.

7. Who is eligible to apply for the programme?

Students who are:

Currently in Class XII, or

Pass-outs from 2024 onwards

are eligible to apply.

8. What is the duration of the programme?

The programme duration is 4 years.

9. Do students study abroad during the programme?

Yes. Students spend one year studying at Illinois Institute of Technology in Chicago as part of the programme.

10. What benefits do students get from studying in two countries?

Students gain:

Global academic exposure

International industry insights

Networking opportunities with global companies

Curriculum – UG Programme in Technology & Business Management (Global)
Year 1 – Foundations of Technology & Business
1. What is the focus of Year 1 in the programme?

Year 1 focuses on building the fundamental knowledge of business, economics, and technology, along with analytical and communication skills.

2. What core subjects are taught in Year 1?

Students study courses such as:

Art of Communication

Intro to PowerPoint & Excel

Legalities in Business

Fundamentals of Accounting

Microeconomics

Macroeconomics

3. What analytical skills do students develop in Year 1?

Students learn analytical skills through courses like:

Problem Solving and Structuring

Fundamentals of Statistics

Data Visualisation using Tableau and SQL

4. Do students learn product and marketing concepts in the first year?

Yes. Students learn:

Introduction to Product Management

Building Marketing Strategies

5. What experiential learning opportunities are available in Year 1?

Students participate in activities such as:

Dropshipping Challenge – building a D2C brand and generating ₹10 lakh or more in revenue

Global Immersion Programme – visiting global innovation hubs like Japan, Singapore, Germany, and Silicon Valley

Shopfloor Internship – gaining hands-on experience in manufacturing and operations

Year 2 – Develop Modern Business Skills
6. What is the focus of Year 2?

Year 2 focuses on modern business skills including product analytics, digital marketing, corporate finance, and venture capital.

7. What management and leadership subjects are taught in Year 2?

Students study subjects such as:

Managerial Behaviour

Introduction to HRM

Corporate Governance

Corporate Strategy

8. What finance and business courses are included in Year 2?

Courses include:

Corporate Finance

Venture Capital and Fintech

Supply Chain & Logistics

9. What technology and analytics skills are taught in Year 2?

Students learn:

Product Analytics

Intro to Machine Learning

Design Thinking & Prototyping

10. What practical experiences do students get in Year 2?

Students participate in activities such as:

Content Creation Challenge – building a digital presence and reaching 5,000+ followers

Corporate Internship – gaining real-world experience in companies

Global Immersion Programme – visiting international business ecosystems

Year 3 – Business Leadership & Innovation
11. What is the focus of Year 3 in the programme?

Year 3 focuses on advanced business strategy, entrepreneurship, and leadership development.

12. What advanced subjects are taught in Year 3?

Students study courses such as:

Founder’s Playbook

Negotiation

Valuations & Financial Modelling

Product Strategy & Innovation

Strategic Brand Management

13. Do students learn about emerging technologies?

Yes. Students explore new-age technologies including Generative AI and prompt engineering.

14. What data and technology skills are taught in Year 3?

Students learn:

Big Data Analytics

Machine Learning applications for business

15. What practical experiences are offered in Year 3?

Students participate in activities such as:

Industry Treks – visiting startups, VC firms, and large companies

Leadership Retreats – intensive leadership training programmes

Venture Initiation Programme – building and pitching startups to real investors

Year 4 – Global Study Experience
16. What happens in Year 4 of the programme?

Students spend the final year studying at Illinois Institute of Technology in Chicago.

17. What degree do students receive after completing the programme?

Students graduate with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from Illinois Tech (USA).

18. What opportunities do students get during the study abroad year?

Students gain:

International academic exposure

Access to global internship networks

Experience studying in a global business hub

19. Why is Chicago important for business education?

Chicago is a major global financial and business hub, providing exposure to multinational companies, startups, and investors.

Fees – UG Programme in Technology & Business Management (Global)
Programme Fees
1. What is the total tuition fee for the Technology & Business Management (Global) programme?

The total tuition fee for the programme is ₹1,14,80,000, excluding additional personal expenses.

2. Is there an admission fee for the programme?

Yes. Students must pay a non-refundable admission fee of ₹1,80,000.

3. What are the tuition fees for each year of the programme?
Year	Tuition Fee
Year 1	₹10,80,000
Year 2	₹15,95,000
Year 3	₹17,85,000
Year 4 (Illinois Tech)	₹70,20,000
4. Why is the fourth-year fee higher than the other years?

The fourth year is completed at Illinois Institute of Technology in Chicago, which includes international tuition fees and global academic resources.

5. How is the fourth-year tuition calculated?

The fourth-year tuition fee is approximately $78,000 converted to INR using an exchange rate of 1:90.

The actual amount may vary based on the exchange rate at the time of study.

6. What is the total programme cost including admission fee?

The total programme cost including the admission fee is ₹1,16,60,000.

Hostel Fees
7. Does the programme provide residential accommodation?

Yes. The programme offers optional hostel accommodation for students in Gurugram during the first three years.

8. What are the hostel accommodation options?

Students can choose between:

Twin Sharing

Single Sharing

9. What is the hostel fee for twin sharing?

The hostel fee for twin sharing accommodation is ₹27,500 per month.

10. What is the hostel fee for single sharing?

The hostel fee for single sharing accommodation is ₹48,500 per month.

11. Does the hostel fee cover the entire programme duration?

No. The hostel fee mentioned covers accommodation in Gurugram for the first three years of the programme.

12. Are electricity charges and taxes included in the hostel fee?

No. Electricity charges and applicable taxes are not included in the hostel fee.
