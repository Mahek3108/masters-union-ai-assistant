## PGP Rise in General Management

The **PGP Rise in General Management** is a **12-month career acceleration programme** designed specifically for **mid-career professionals** who want to transition into leadership roles, expand their management capabilities, or reset their career trajectory.

The programme combines **online and in-person weekend learning**, enabling working professionals to continue their jobs while upgrading their business and leadership skills.

---

* Online sessions
* In-person weekend classes
* Industry interactions and global immersions

**12 months**

**May 2026 (Tentative)**

The programme is intended for **working professionals** seeking to advance into leadership, strategic management, or entrepreneurial roles.

PGP Rise in General Management
Curriculum Overview

The PGP Rise in General Management curriculum is structured into four progressive modules, each designed to strengthen leadership capability, business strategy understanding, and practical execution skills. The programme blends in-class learning with real-world business challenges, residencies, and venture-building experiences.

Participants develop expertise across finance, marketing, leadership, technology, and entrepreneurship, while also working on real projects and business initiatives.

Module 1: Leadership Foundation

The first module builds the fundamental business and leadership skills required to understand organisations, markets, and decision-making processes.

Business Finance & Fintech

Participants learn how financial systems support business operations and decision-making. Topics include:

Reading and analysing financial statements

Budget allocation and cost management

Financial planning for business growth

Sales & Marketing

This section focuses on how ideas evolve into market-ready products and brands.

Key topics include:

Identifying viable business ideas and market opportunities

Developing compelling brand narratives

Understanding consumer decision-making processes

Leadership & Self Development

Participants develop core leadership and interpersonal skills necessary to manage teams and influence stakeholders.

Learning areas include:

Public speaking and persuasive communication

Building a strong personal brand

Managing people and workplace relationships

Understanding economic behaviour in markets

Product & Technology

Students learn how analytical tools and statistics support business decision-making and product development.

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Participants are introduced to generative AI and learn how to effectively use prompt engineering techniques to leverage AI tools in business workflows.

Tools Training

Practical tool training includes advanced usage of Microsoft Excel for data analysis and decision-making.

Career Preparation

Participants begin building career readiness through resume development and professional positioning.

Experiential Learning

Two experiential activities accompany this module:

Campus Residency – Orientation

Participants visit campus for networking sessions with fellow professionals and industry experts, creating early professional connections within the cohort.

Dropshipping Challenge

Participants launch a real online business by:

Building an e-commerce website

Running marketing campaigns

Managing supply chains

Competing to generate maximum revenue

This activity introduces participants to entrepreneurship and market experimentation.

Module 2: Growth & Scaling

The second module focuses on expanding business operations and scaling products, organisations, and leadership impact.

Finance & Fintech

Participants explore advanced financial management topics such as:

Corporate financial management

Emerging financial technologies including decentralised finance (DeFi) and cryptocurrencies

Sales & Marketing

Students learn how to understand customer behaviour and market dynamics.

Topics include:

Consumer psychology and behaviour analysis

Market research techniques for strategic decision-making

Leadership & Self Development

Leadership training focuses on managing complex organisational environments.

Participants learn:

Stakeholder management strategies

Competitive strategy development

Understanding national economic systems

Personal productivity and behavioural optimisation

Product & Technology

Participants learn how to design and build AI-powered products.

AI & Machine Learning

Students develop skills in building simple digital applications using no-code development tools.

Tool Training

Data visualisation and business intelligence are taught through Power BI, enabling participants to interpret complex business data.

Career Preparation

Participants learn how to build a professional presence on LinkedIn and develop an effective online professional identity.

Experiential Learning

Venture Initiation Programme (VIP) – Pre-Seed Stage

Participants pitch their early startup ideas to entrepreneurs and investors as part of the Venture Initiation Programme, competing for potential early-stage funding.

Campus Residency

Participants bring their previously created dropshipping businesses into a physical marketplace environment, where they interact with real customers and receive feedback from industry mentors.

Module 3: Advanced Leadership & Impact

The third module deepens leadership capabilities and strategic decision-making skills while preparing participants for entrepreneurship and product innovation.

Finance & Fintech

Participants study advanced financial concepts including:

Financial modelling techniques

IPO processes and corporate valuation

Sales & Marketing

Participants explore digital growth strategies, including:

Leveraging social media algorithms for brand growth

Product & Technology

The module teaches how to design habit-forming products that drive long-term user engagement.

Leadership & Self Development

Leadership development focuses on strategic thinking and team management.

Key topics include:

Using mental models for complex decision-making

Running effective meetings and motivating teams

Entering and expanding into new markets successfully

AI & Machine Learning

Participants learn data analysis techniques to support business strategy and decision-making.

Tool Training

Participants gain practical skills in UI/UX design using Figma, enabling them to prototype digital products.

Career Preparation

Students learn how to build a professional portfolio showcasing their work and achievements.

Experiential Learning

Venture Initiation Programme – MVP Stage

Participants validate their startup ideas and build a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) while competing for ₹15+ lakh in potential pre-seed funding.

Global Immersion Programme

Participants attend international business immersions that include:

Workshops with global C-suite leaders

Industry visits across international companies

Learning sessions at top global business schools such as INSEAD and SDA Bocconi

The final module focuses on applying leadership knowledge to real-world business scenarios and preparing participants for leadership roles or entrepreneurship.

Topics include:

Participants learn how to optimise product performance using data-driven insights.

Tool Training

Career Preparation

Participants prepare for career transitions through:

Interview preparation

Professional storytelling

Experiential Learning

Content Creator Challenge

Participants learn modern marketing techniques by building their own digital influencer brand and growing audiences on social media platforms.

Participants demonstrate product-market fit (PMF) and develop go-to-market strategies while pitching their ventures to investors for potential seed funding.

Application Timeline & Admissions Process

The admissions process for the PGP Rise in General Management programme is designed to evaluate applicants based on their professional background, leadership potential, and career aspirations. The process consists of three key stages: application submission, interview evaluation, and final admission decision.

Application Rounds
Round 1

Application Deadline: 20 March 2026
Interview Period: February – March 2026
Application Fee: INR 500
Status: Active

Applicants must submit their application before the deadline to be considered in this round.

Stage 1: Complete the Application

Applicants begin the process by filling out an online application form for the programme.

Required Information

The application form requires applicants to submit the following information:

Personal details

Professional details and career history

Updated resume

Applicants are also required to submit a short essay explaining their motivation and goals.

Essay Submission Options

Candidates can choose one of the following formats:

Video Essay

Duration: 2 minutes

A short introduction describing the applicant’s career journey, aspirations, and reasons for applying to the programme.

Written Essay

Maximum length: 200 words

A concise explanation of the applicant’s professional goals and interest in the programme.

This stage allows the admissions committee to evaluate the applicant’s motivation, communication ability, and career direction.

Stage 2: Online Interview

Shortlisted candidates are invited to participate in an online interview.

Interview Purpose

The interview serves two objectives:

Allow the admissions committee to better understand the applicant’s professional background and leadership potential.

Give applicants the opportunity to learn more about the programme and its structure.

Interview Panel

The interview panel typically includes:

Faculty members and academic experts

Industry leaders and practitioners

Members of the Masters’ Union core admissions team

During the interview, candidates may be assessed on:

Professional experience and career achievements

Leadership capabilities and decision-making approach

Career goals and programme fit

Communication and problem-solving abilities

Stage 3: Admission Decision

After the interview process is completed, the admissions team reviews the applicant’s profile.

The admissions committee evaluates:

Application form

Essay submission

Interview performance

Professional background and leadership potential

Based on this holistic evaluation, the committee makes the final admission decision.

Applicants are informed about the result through official email communication.

Decision Timeline

The entire admissions process typically takes up to four weeks from the application deadline.
