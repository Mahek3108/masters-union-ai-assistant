How do I decide which programme to choose, or which programme is right
If you are dealing with complex family dynamics or need
long-term business transformation, leadership growth, and legacy building, OPM is the right
choice. It is a 12-month programme for those focused on sustainable growth, succession, and
If there are not too many complications in your family dynamics, your primary pain point is
sales or business acceleration, and you want to make a shorter commitment of your time, then
9-month BAP is a better program for you.
What is the main outcome difference?
12 months OPM = holistic, sustainable transformation and leadership
9 months BAP = fast, practical sales and process gains
Will there be the same level of rigour of practical application in
Yes. Both programs include assignments and Capstone to ensure
whatever is taught in the class is thoroughly applied in your company. A pressing problem
that your organisation is currently facing is solved with the help of Capstone, with one on
one coaching by faculty and mentors.
Will I miss out a lot by choosing the BAP?
It's all about what your need is. Yes, you will miss out on
some aspects like in-depth modules for family, self, patriarch, non family CXOs. Also,
international immersions and Fractional Board. Nevertheless, if your current pressing need
is revenue growth, then you can go for BAP.
Is it worth doing the 12-month program if my goal is only sales
It is better to compare all aspects of both programs, what
different and additional you are getting in each, and then decide.
Can freshers apply for the 9-month BAP Certificate?
Not recommended. Ideal for those with at
least 2–3 years of business/operations experience.
Is the 9-month BAP program also designed for family businesses?
Both OPM and BAP are designed keeping
needs of family business owners and promoters.
Is the 9-month BAP course a subset of OPM?
No. It is a different program. While some
sessions are similar to OPM, there are many sessions in BAP which are not taught in OPM. See

scholarships, offer of bringing a family member for free, international immersions,

Having completed BAP, can I upgrade myself to OPM by doing additional
Yes, it is possible by doing additional
modules and international immersions. However, we would like to understand your business
objectives and then recommend the best way forward.
Can I upgrade from BAP to OPM during the program?
Yes, by completing additional modules and
paying incremental fees. However, the total cost of upgrading through this pathway will be
higher than pursuing OPM directly from the beginning. We recommend carefully evaluating your
long term objectives before making this decision.

The in-person business simulation will
require students to role-play as a CXO of a company and solve its business challenges in a

discussion for 45 minutes followed by a presentation (without any slides).
