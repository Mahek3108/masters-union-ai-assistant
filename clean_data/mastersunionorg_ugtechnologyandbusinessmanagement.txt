creativity, communication, and teamwork skills. They will be participating in a group
discussion for 45 minutes followed by a presentation (without any slides).

Does Masters’ Union offer scholarships?
At Masters’ Union we believe that financial constraints should not pose any hindrance to students’ academic aspirations. We have merit and need-based scholarships for all deserving candidates. Students scoring above 35 out of 45 marks in the Masters’ Union Scholarship Aptitude test get a scholarship which can range from 10% - 25% of the tuition fee depending on the student’s performance in the Scholarship (Aptitude)Test and the overall profile of the applicant. For candidates keen on scholarship, we encourage students to submit their application early to allow for our admission counsellors to thoroughly review their scholarship case before presenting it to the admissions committee at the time of evaluation.

What will I graduate with at the end of the programme?
Upon completing the programme, you will be awarded a Professional Certificate for Undergraduate Programme in Tech & Business Management by Masters' Union. Additionally, you will have the option to enrol in IIM Bangalore to pursue a BBA in Digital Business and Entrepreneurship, provided you clear all the exams and the eligibility criteria as set by the IIM Bangalore.
What is the difference between the Masters' Union certification and the IIM Bangalore degree?
The Masters' Union certification focuses on an industry-integrated, tech-driven curriculum designed for business management professionals. The IIM Bangalore (BBA) degree is a traditional academic qualification offered with standard coursework and university examinations.
What makes the Masters’ Union Programme a better choice than earning the same degree from IIM Bangalore?
While the IIM Bangalore degree provides a strong academic foundation,Masters' Union offers real-world exposure from day one. Through outclasses and internships starting in Year 1, students gain hands-on experience with industry leaders from top companies like Microsoft, Google, and Zomato. This early exposure, combined with rigorous academic training, ensures that our students are not only academically strong but also industry-ready, with the highest CTC touching ₹61.5 LPA and an average CTC of ₹28.5 LPA, securing top-tier job offers even before graduation.
Is this an online degree, is it authentic?
Yes, the degree is awarded through IIM Bangalore’s online platform, making it an online degree. However, it holds the same recognition and value as any full-time IIM Bangalore degree.
How well is this degree recognised for higher studies abroad?
The degree awarded by IIM Bangalore is highly respected and recognised for higher studies abroad. With IIM Bangalore’s global reputation and the rigor of its curriculum, the degree holds significant international credibility, opening doors to top global universities and MBA programs for further education.
Can I still pursue the Masters' Union certification if I don’t enrol in the IIM Bangalore degree?
Yes, you can still complete the Masters' Union Undergraduate Programme in Tech and Business Management and earn the certification even if you choose not to enrol in the IIM Bangalore degree.
