What is a PG Programme in Sports Management & Gaming (SMG)?
A 16-month full-time postgraduate programme blending Masters’
Union’s business foundation with hands-on specialisations in sports management, e-sports,
What will I learn in the programme?
Learn to run sports leagues, manage athletes, and build gaming
startups in a rapidly evolving sports and digital ecosystem. Learn directly from top
faculty, CXOs, ex-athletes, and sports entrepreneurs through live case studies, mentorship,
and industry-led workshops. Our “learn by doing” curriculum shapes future leaders at the
intersection of sports, analytics, media, and entrepreneurship.
What is the programme structure?
The curriculum spans 6 terms across 16 months
Terms 1-2: building business fundamentals
Terms 3-5: mastering industry-specific skills
Term 6: Live project with a sports or gaming organisation
What will be the batch size of the class?
We will limit the batch size to a maximum of 60 students to
maximize individual attention from masters, and have an impactful teacher-student ratio.
What is the eligibility criteria?
The PGP in Sports Management & Gaming is
open to candidates who have completed their undergraduate degree or will graduate by April
2026 onwards. Ideal applicants include fresh graduates, early-career professionals (0-5
years), and entrepreneurs in sports tech or e-sports.
